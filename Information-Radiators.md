## Task-11: Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

- Assigned To : Lalith Adithya Reddy Avuthu.
- Explain : 1. Lack of Stakeholder Engagement:
            - Risk: Stakeholders may not actively engage or provide timely feedback, leading to misalignment with project goals.
            - Mitigation: Regular stakeholder meetings and updates to ensure active participation. Clear communication channels established for feedback and discussions.

            2. Technical Challenges with Technology Stack:
            - Risk: Technical issues or limitations with the chosen technology stack (React.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL) may arise, impacting development progress.
            - Mitigation: Conduct thorough research and feasibility studies before implementing technologies. Continuous monitoring and evaluation of technical challenges with proactive problem-solving approaches.

            3. Integration Complexity:
            - Risk: Complex integration requirements between modules may result in delays or compatibility issues.
            - Mitigation: Plan integration points and data exchange formats early in the project. Conduct thorough testing of integrations to identify and resolve issues promptly.

            4. Dependency on External Services:
            - Risk: Reliance on external services (e.g., third-party APIs for email notifications) may result in service disruptions or changes impacting project timelines.
            - Mitigation: Identify alternative service providers or fallback mechanisms. Implement error handling and monitoring for external service dependencies.

            5. Scope Creep:
            - Risk: Project scope may expand beyond initial requirements, leading to increased development time and resource allocation.
            - Mitigation: Establish clear project scope and requirements documentation. Regularly review and prioritize features to prevent scope creep. Implement change management processes for evaluating and approving scope changes.

            6. Resource Constraints:
            - Risk: Limited availability of resources may impact project delivery.
            - Mitigation: Conduct resource planning and allocation upfront. Identify potential bottlenecks and allocate resources accordingly. Communicate resource constraints early to stakeholders and explore options for resource augmentation if needed.

            7. Lack of User Adoption:
            - Risk: Users may not adopt the system as expected, leading to low engagement and impact on project success.
            - Mitigation: Involve end-users early in the design and development process through user testing and feedback sessions. Provide comprehensive training and support resources to facilitate user adoption.

            8. Regulatory Compliance Issues:
                - Risk: Failure to comply with relevant regulations (e.g., data privacy laws) may result in legal consequences and reputational damage.
                - Mitigation: Stay informed about regulatory requirements and ensure the project adheres to applicable laws and standards. Conduct regular compliance checks and audits to identify and address any non-compliance issues.
