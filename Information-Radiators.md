Key performance indicators (KPIs) for the project

The Project Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are specialized quantitative metrics used to evaluate the effectiveness/success or outcome achievement of the project during the course of implementation. KPIs are merely the quantities that tell us how well the things we have different dimensions of the project are working, thus enabling the stakeholders to know how far the project has performed, which areas need to be improved, and what decisions are better to make.

KPI's for the entire project: 

Overall Project Completion Time: Make a time estimation for the whole part of the project starting from the first moment of the project development to its completion.

Stakeholder Satisfaction: Sourcing for feedback from all the stakeholders (admin, donors, users) either through online or offline forums to gauge their satisfaction with the properly functioning and easy to operate app.

Accuracy of Data Entry: Oversee the reliability of data entered into the system, particularly donor details, programs selected and mechanisms of donation.

System Performance: Measure the system's performance, including response time, uptime, and overall stability. Assess how these indicators impact the overall reliability of the system.

Program Impact Assessment: Determine the workings and effect of the program on beneficiaries which includes the number of individuals served, materials distributed, and results oriented.

Email Engagement Metrics: Monitor the behavior of donors by measuring open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates after delivering emails to see how they respond.

Security Compliance: Safeguard the data through adhering to the data protection regulations and protecting information as an industry standard.


Module-Specific Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

Authentication Module:
User Registration Rate: Measure the rate of new admin sign-ups to assess the system's adoption and user engagement.
Password Recovery Success Rate: Keep track whether those with password recovery requests are able to access the system and get all the needed user support.

Email Module:
Email Opt-in Rate: Calculate the ratio of the contributed email addresses that have opted for notifications to check communication strategies’ effectiveness.
Email Triggering Accuracy: Assess the precision of automatic email triggering taking in account different types of donation and employee assignments.
Email Engagement Metrics: Track the numbers of open emails, click through rates and response rates to conduct donor engagement and effectiveness of the communication.

Admin - Checkin Donors and Donations Module:
Donor Registration Accuracy: Evaluate the precision of the inputted donor registration data in order to make sure that the system has quality data.
Donation Assignment Time: Measure the time taken to assign donations to programs to ensure timely processing and allocation.
Data Search and Update Efficiency: Verify the efficiency of the search and update functions for the donors and donations records to streamline administrative tasks.

Admin - Program Checkin for Opted-in Users Module:
Program Assignment Accuracy: Assess the accuracy of program assignments for materials to ensure proper allocation and tracking.
Bulk Assignment Efficiency: Measure the efficiency of bulk assignment processes for donations to improve administrative productivity.
Image Upload Success Rate: Track the success rate of image uploads for programs and donations to ensure proper documentation and reporting.
