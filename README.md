# Automated Location Information:

Users input a zip code, and the system automatically populates the corresponding city and state, enhancing data accuracy.
# Unique Donor and Material IDs:

Unique IDs are generated for donors and materials using a formula incorporating material type, date, and zip code, ensuring systematic identification.
# Dynamic Material Addition:

A user-friendly "Add" button enables donors to easily input multiple materials, providing flexibility in the donation process.
# additional Donor Information:

Donors can provide additional information if they are interested in updates on the donation life cycle and donation programs.
Purpose:

Clearly state that the form is for material donation registration, streamlining the donation process.
Key Objectives:

Streamline donation process.
Collect donor information for inventory management.
Provide a way for donors to express their intent to donate materials.
Information Needed:

Donor's contact details: Full name, email, phone, address.
Material details: Type, condition, quantity, comments.
Opt-in for donation journey updates.
Questions about volunteering or supporting programs
